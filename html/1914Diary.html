1914 Jan 12-29

Image Id: 001-0471
Transcription
  • Mon Jan 26:  Ada Wilson died today.

1914 Jun 20-Jul 14

Image Id: 001-0479
Transcription
  • Wed Jun 24:  Granddaughter Miriam Ella Sisson born.

1914 Jul 15-Aug 3

Image Id: 001-0480
Transcription
  • Mon Jul 20:  Went to Mt Vernon and filed for County Comm. Grant hauling in hay. Strong west wind.
  • Sat Aug 01:  Nice day. Went to Pioneer meeting at Sedro Woolley. Nice crowd and a good time. Made about 54 miles today. Took Chas. W. Beale and Mrs Whitney. Mr Beale is the oldest resident Pioneer of the Skagit Co and is 83 years old. Got home at 6:30 with any trouble.

1914 Aug 4-28

Image Id: 001-0481
Transcription
  • Tue Aug 04:  War in Europe

1914 Aug 29-Sep 19

Image Id: 001-0482
Transcription
  • Sat Aug 29:  At home and downtown. Watching the Asphalt machine and the spreading of the A. W.J. Thompson, Ex Com, killed in the Auto accident last night. Another victim of booze..
  • Tue Sep 08:  Rain most all day. Primary election Day. Very light vote. was nominated for Co. Com.

1914 Oct 9-30

Image Id: 001-0484
Transcription
  • Tue Oct 27:  65 today. Reps at Sedro Woolley. Self at home tending to my business

1914 Oct 31-Nov 23

Image Id: 001-0485
Transcription
  • Tue Nov 03:  Rainy day & Election Day. Out with car to Summit Park & Whitney Island for voters. Down to the Polls in the Eve.
  • Wed Nov 04:  State gone "dry". All Repbs in County elected except Neal Bradsberry & Cogswell. Downtown & to Bessner's in the Eve. Self elected Co. Com.
