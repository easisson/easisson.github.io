1931 Jan 1-17

Image Id: 003-0109
Transcription
  • Thu Jan 01:  Clowdy, but no rain. At home. Very quiet New Year day. Temp 40. Ida miserable and has a pain in her head.
  • Fri Jan 02:  Clowdy with light rains. Downtown in the P.M. to pay bills. Frank took Miriam home & home the bal. of the day.
  • Sat Jan 03:  Clowdy. Wrights went to Seattle. I went with Bessner to Mr. Rudene's funeral at Mt. Vernon. Large funeral. Another good pioneer gone.
  • Sun Jan 04:  Clowdy Temp 48. Ida miserable ever since Christmas. Went Church. Darrell called this A.M. Wrights went to Bellingham with a new Buick. Francis drove one. Heard via wire that Donald's wife has a permature baby boy born this A.M. [margin: Great grand parents.]
  • Mon Jan 05:  Ida down to see Dr. Shaw with Mrs. Young. At home all day. Frank in B. Weather mild. Light rains.
  • Tue Jan 06:  Clowdy. Went to the farm. Got potatos & parsnips. Wright in B.
  • Wed Jan 07:  Pleasant Fine Day for winter. Home reading. Feet & legs no better. Wright in B.
  • Thu Jan 08:  Clowdy. At home all day. Wrights in Seattle.
  • Fri Jan 09:  Clowdy Temp 40. Down town in the P.M. Paid for Cetrycue & A. American. Card from Donald. Folks getting sleep OK. Frank to Seattle for a car.
  • Sat Jan 10:  Rainy Day. At home A.M. went to show P.M. "Lightnin" with Abbey & Campbell. Ida in bed, bladder trouble &c. Frank in Bellingham.
  • Sun Jan 11:  Rain last night 1/2 in since yes. A.M. Went to church A.M.
  • Mon Jan 12:  Quite Pleasant. Went to C.C. Luncheon. Big argument over new Avon road. Endorsed by the Chamber. Grant & folks here. Frank in ??? Bellingham.
  • Tue Jan 13:  Clowdy. S.E. wind. Wrights got a new lot of furniture. Frank went to Seattle.
  • Wed Jan 14:  Strong S.E. wind & some rain. Went to Mr Daggetts funeral in Mt. Vernon. Frank in Seattle. Mr Johnson came to A with me. D.A.R. Meeting here tonight.
  • Thu Jan 15:  Clowdy. Downtown in the P.M. Called on the Judge. Frank in Seattle. Temp 44.
  • Fri Jan 16:  Clowdy. Temp 44. At home all day. Frank in Seattle till night. Donald arrived from Cal. Ida miserable. Chet Leamer here this A.M.
  • Sat Jan 17:  Temp 43. Semi Pleasant. At home A.M. Went to Avon & took Pete Miller in the P.M. Nature project progressing. Digging ditch at Summit Park. A lot of pipe on the ground there and at Whitney & O????ity. Frank & Donald in Bellingham.

1931 Jan 18-31

Image Id: 003-0110
Transcription
  • Sun Jan 18:  [margin: Emma Tillinghast died.] Pleasant Temp 32. Went to church. P.M. Went to Helen W. Shipp's funeral at Burlington. Very large funeral.
  • Mon Jan 19:  Pleasant. At home & downtown. Gas Co. ditching in the ally. Frank & Donald in Bellingham. Sent a letter to Eva Reed.
  • Tue Jan 20:  Pleasant, Beautiful day & quite warm. Went to Emma Tillinghast's funeral and took Mrs. Whitney and Abbey. A very large funeral. Rev. Simmons officiated.
  • Wed Jan 21:  Clowdy & moist. At home & down town. Francis has a Buick Coupe. Don in Bellingham. Frank in Seattle.
  • Thu Jan 22:  Rainy. Went to Seattle with Frank and consulted with Dr. Palmer. Home at 8 P.M.
  • Fri Jan 23:  Rainy or showery. Gas Company filled in the pipe ditch in the ally. Went to the farm. Oats & hay sell at $20.00 & $15.00 per ton. Frank at Bellingham.
  • Sat Jan 24:  Pleasant. In town in the P.M. Frank in Bellingham. Donald here at night.
  • Sun Jan 25:  Clowdy & windy. Went to church A.M. Donald went to Bellingham. Temp 46. Warmest Jan for many years.
  • Mon Jan 26:  Clowdy. No rain. Went to CC lunch. Home reading; the balance of the times. Frank in Bellingham. Don here this eve.
  • Tue Jan 27:  Rainly. At home A.M. Pearl & boys here last evening. Ida to see Mrs. Subeshire(sp?). Margerite also and I took her home. Frank in Bellingham, Don also.
  • Wed Jan 28:  Beautiful day temp 63. Warmest day I ever saw in January. Ida & I went to Wilson's. No body home. Called at Leatherwoods & at the farm for lunch & went to Burlington to see about dresses. No luck. Frank home from Seattle this Eve.
  • Thu Jan 29:  Fine day. At home. Downtown P.M. Temp at noon 58. Frank in Bellingham.
  • Fri Jan 30:  Pleasant. Took Ida to Frank Whites. Then went to the farm for lunch. In the P.M. went to La Conner and called on Bailey at the Seed store. Frank in Bellingham.
  • Sat Jan 31:  Pleasant home A.M. Went with Ida and Margerite Young to show "Reducing". Frank in B'm. Ida & I called on the Summers & Mr. Summer in bed. Thurs endeth the warmest January on record. Darrell brot a load of wood from Carl Johnson with Grant's truck. 3 ricks.

1931 Sep 16-30

Image Id: 003-0126
Transcription
  • Thu Sep 17:  Grant took us to Seattle. I at Virginia Mason Hospital and Ida went to McGinniss' at night.
  • Fri Sep 18:  Dr. Flothow operated on me for foot and leg trouble but to no effect. I did not enjoy the experience.

1931 Oct 17-27

Image Id: 003-0128
Transcription
  • Sat Oct 17:  Colvin, Pearl & Nels came this P.M. and Colvin bought the Ford. I hated to see it go but it is no use to me in my condition.
  • Sun Oct 18:  Donald & Dorothy and son Jimmy came at 12:45. all looking well.
  • Thu Oct 22:  Mrs. June Burn and Mother Mrs. Adrris from Bellingham here to "get" some of my diary.
  • Fri Oct 23:  Suffering as usual in bed all day.
  • Tue Oct 27:  82 today. 40 visitors today on birthday, received 44 birthday cards. All names are entered.
