1927 Oct 23-Nov 10

Image Id: 003-0031
Transcription
  • Sun Oct 23:  Rain this AM. Pleasant P.M. Went Pres church A.M. Ida & I went to Frank Whites in the P.M. Listened to Seattle sermons in the eve.
  • Mon Oct 24:  No rain clowdy. In town all day. Donald & wife came this eve. The scare letter from George about Donald came this P.M.
  • Tue Oct 25:  Fine day. Went to the farm, Ida also & Donalds folks. Grant in Olympia. Donald & wife at Pearl's to night.
  • Wed Oct 26:  Pleasant. Downtown in the P.M. & visited Mr Knapp.
  • Thu Oct 27:  Rain last night. Pleasant P.M. Went with Abbey out to E. Howard's to a stump removing demonstration. Am 78 today and feel about like it.
  • Fri Oct 28:  Showery & windy. At home A.M. Down at the Judge's office in P.M.
  • Sat Oct 29:  Clowdy & rain at night. At home & up to the school ground with Frank. They came at 11 A.M. Frank, Nettie & Francis.
  • Sun Oct 30:  Clear & pleasant but cold w temp 43. Celebrating my birthday. Pearl, Colvin, Merlin, Hilda, Darrell & Miriam & the Wrights here. Went to Pres Church in the eve.
  • Mon Oct 31:  Frost 35. At home & down town in the P.M. [margin: Grant home with an elk from Eastern Washington.]
  • Tue Nov 01:  Cold wet rain all day. Down town in the P.M. to get radio battery.
  • Wed Nov 02:  Henry McLean died. Misty all day. down in the P.M.
  • Thu Nov 03:  Dito yesterday. Wrote to Henry Ford.
  • Fri Nov 04:  Clowdy & rainy. At home all day. Grant in town but did no visit us.
  • Sat Nov 05:  Clowdy no rain. Downtown with Ida this A.M. Big F. Ball game today. Stanford vs U of W. S. wins 7-13.
  • Sun Nov 06:  Very Pleasant. Rain A.M. Went to Presby church in A.M. & S.S. In the P.M. went out to Whitney's.
  • Mon Nov 07:  Clowdy & windy. Rain A.M. At house & downtown.
  • Tue Nov 08:  Clowdy. Temp 40 S.E. wind. Went to the Ranch. Ida also. Had some of the elk for dinner.
  • Wed Nov 09:  Cool & clowdy. Spading garden & choring, also set the wood shed. Downtown in the P.M.
  • Thu Nov 10:  Rainy A.M. Temp 30. At home all day. Reading & listening to radio.

1927 Nov 11-26

Image Id: 003-0032
Transcription
  • Fri Nov 11:  Armistice Day. Rain all day. Got a load of wood (box) this A.M. early. I put it in the shed. Ida not well. Radio gave us some fine programs.
  • Sat Nov 12:  Rain all day. Temp 38. At home. Downtown an hour. Ida not well.
  • Sun Nov 13:  Showery Temp 40. Went to Presby in the A.M. At home the balance of the day.
  • Mon Nov 14:  Showery. Temp 48. Went to C.C. luncheon. Attended police court.
  • Tue Nov 15:  Rain after 11 A.M. Temp 40. Ida & I went to the farm. Home at 3. It has rained every day this month.
  • Wed Nov 16:  Temp 56 at noon. Showery. At home & downtown. Frank Whites folks here this eve. Listened to the Victoria Play.
  • Thu Nov 17:  Light rain this A.M. In the P.M. Ida & I went and called on Bob Woodburn's folks. He is still in bed after 6 weeks with a broken hip.
  • Fri Nov 18:  Rainy. Temp 48. In town all day. Down to Abbey's office in the P.M.
  • Sat Nov 19:  Clowdy & just a little rain. Down with Ida in the P.M. Mrs. Bradley returned from Portland this eve with a cold.
  • Sun Nov 20:  very pleasant & light rain. Went to Pres Church A.M. & P.M.
  • Mon Nov 21:  Clowdy with light rains. Downtown in the P.M. at police court. In the eve went to the Presby supper & program. Wells, Jarvis & Thompson spoke.
  • Tue Nov 22:  Clowdy & little rain. Downtown in the P.M.
  • Wed Nov 23:  Very pleasant & a light sprinkle. Went to Wilsons & stopped at the farm. Mrs. Bradley went as far as McCulloughs.
  • Thu Nov 24:  Rain this A.M. Went to the Union Thank meeting. Home the balance of the day. Ida coming down with cold. Jessie home this eve.
  • Fri Nov 25:  Clowdy but no rain. Downtown in the P.M. The Wrights came at 9 P.M.
  • Sat Nov 26:  Clowdy. Temp 33 Frost. The Wrights here & Merriam also. Rain at night.

1927 Nov 28-Dec 15

Image Id: 003-0033
Transcription
  • Mon Nov 28:  Showery & rain last night. At home & downtown with Ida.
  • Tue Nov 29:  Clowdy & light rains. Downtown A.M. & P.M. Nothing unusual. Temp 42.
  • Wed Nov 30:  Clowdy some rain. Took Ida down to Dodges to get fitted for glasses.
  • Thu Dec 01:  Rain nearly all day. 1 1/3 inch since last night at 5 oclock. Took Ida to get a Collins bath.
  • Fri Dec 02:  Rain early this A.M. but clear most of the day with west wind. Skagit pretty high. Saw Ford pictures of the new car.
  • Sat Dec 03:  Pleasant. Temp 40. Downtown with Ida for bath.
  • Sun Dec 04:  Clowdy but no rain. Went to S.S. & church A.M. to Mr Glass' funeral in the P.M. & to the Bellingham Cantata in the Eve at the Pres Church. [margin: ]
  • Mon Dec 05:  Coller went to the Ranch and to Mount Vernon Kiwanis with Grant. Ida at the Ranch. Ben Palmer died.
  • Tue Dec 06:  Temp 33. Ground frozen some. Took Ida to get her new glasses. Went to Abbey's for potatos.
  • Wed Dec 07:  Temp 32. Downtown this AM. Grant here to supper. Heard the Play from Victoria.
  • Thu Dec 08:  Rain last night & same today. Took Ida to get her bath this P.M.
  • Fri Dec 09:  Cold with north wind. Temp 26 and one inch of snow last night. 24 at 10 PM. I went to Elks Play. Good.
  • Sat Dec 10:  Clear & cold. At home tending fires. Temp 23. Ranch 16. Downtown in the P.M.
  • Sun Dec 11:  Temp 26. Strong S wind & snow. Went to the Preby Ch A.M. Listened to M.A. Mathews in the eve by radio.
  • Mon Dec 12:  Temp 30. Rain & snow last night. At home & downtown in the Eve.
  • Tue Dec 13:  Pleasant. Temp 30. Took Ida downtown this P.M. Mrs. Bradley in Mt. Vernon. Streets & walks covered with ice.
  • Wed Dec 14:  Temp 30 Clowdy. At home & down town. Roads icy and slippery.
  • Thu Dec 15:  Snow last night & rain this P.M. Took Ida for her bath this P.M. Temp 32 this AM. 40 to night.

1927 Dec 16-31

Image Id: 003-0034
Transcription
  • Fri Dec 16:  Clowdy and some warmer. 34-42. Downtown with Ida in the P.M.
  • Sat Dec 17:  Temp 32. Clowdy. Went to the ranch in the P.M. Ida also. Lot of ice on the Bay View road.
  • Sun Dec 18:  Temp 30. Clowdy. went to the Presby Church & out to Whitneys in P.M. to see Edith & husband who are to start for Arizona tomorrow.
  • Mon Dec 19:  Light rain. Temp 36. At home & downtown with Ida in the P.M.
  • Tue Dec 20:  Temp 38. Beautiful day. Went & got a Christmas tree A.M. Rains in the P.M.
  • Wed Dec 21:  Temp 34. At home & downtown.
  • Thu Dec 22:  Temp 32. Toook Ida down for here bath. Hickman the murderer caught. Grant in Anacortes. Clowdy.
  • Fri Dec 23:  Temp 40. Nice pleasant. Ida not well. Downtown in the P.M.
  • Sat Dec 24:  Temp 40. Clowdy. The wrights came this eve. except Frank who is not back from Juneau. Mrs Bradley went to the Samish.
  • Sun Dec 25:  Pleasant. Went to Church P. A.M. Had a fine Christmas gathering and a fine dinner. All hands here except John & Carroll. The Whites & Youngs came a while in the Evening.
  • Mon Dec 26:  Showery. The Wrights here. Francis went home with Grant's folks. Down town in the P.M.
  • Tue Dec 27:  Showery temp 40 Mrs Bradley came back. The Wrights here yet. All out to Whitneys last evening.
  • Wed Dec 28:  Rain A.M. Cool at night. Wrights & we went to Grants for lunch. Home at 6 P.M. Temp 32.
  • Thu Dec 29:  Temp 20. Strong No. wind all night & today. The Wrights & Ida went out to Whites to dinner. I have a cold & staid home & tended fires & my nose. Frank & Nettie to Basket B. game.
  • Fri Dec 30:  Temp 15 Clear & cold. At home all day. Ida had another mineral bath. Wrights at Fosters for dinner.
  • Sat Dec 31:  Temp 20 Cold east wind. At home all day, doing nothing but got in wood & kept fires. Cold about the same. This endeth 1927.
