1908 May 17-Jun 6

Image Id: 001-0358
Transcription
  • Thu May 21:  I went with Frank to Bellingham & saw seven Battleships viz the "Connecticut" the "Kansas" the "Vermont" the "Rhode Island" The "New Jersey". & the "Georgia"

1908 Aug 20-Sep 5

Image Id: 001-0363
Transcription
  • Fri Aug 21:  Cutting oats. Weather fine. Crew same as yesterday. Self went to Mt Vernon on subpeona of the state tax Commission. went with Bessner in his auto. Shock baled hay selling at $8.00 to $9.00. Nearly everybody cutting oats.

1908 Sep 6-24

Image Id: 001-0364
Transcription
  • Tue Sep 08:  Primary Elections. Self on the board as Inspector. The machine started at 8 and got out 1300 sks. Self in B.V. till midnight.

1908 Oct 13-30

Image Id: 001-0366
Transcription
  • Tue Oct 27:  59 today.

1908 Oct 31-Nov 14

Image Id: 001-0367
Transcription
  • Tue Nov 03:  Presidential Election. Went to B.V. to take tickets & vote. Johnson went and too Charley & Prince to get their shoes taken off. Johnson hailing manure in the P.M. Heard Taft was elected at 10 P.M.

1908 Nov 15-Dec 4Duplicate of next

Image Id: 001-0368
Transcription
  • Thu Dec 03:  Came home on the wheel. Clear & cold. Johnson hauling dirt. the new phonograph came and I set it up.

1908 Dec 5-25

Image Id: 001-0370
Transcription
  • Sat Dec 05:  Johnson choring & hailing dirt. Self went to Anacortes & took the old phonograph to Timber Bros. was up to Mrs. Whitneys to dinner. Don & Grant came this P.M. Ira start to bring June home & she got sick & was sick all night. Had a Dr. from Mt Vernon.
  • Mon Dec 14:  Not doing much but chores. Ida came home. John Wilson also got home from Idaho. Grant went to Anacortes.
  • Tue Dec 22:  Self went to La Conner. Got a Bank Certificate of Deposit for $1000. To draw interest at 4% from today.

1908 Dec 25-Jan 9, 1909

Image Id: 001-0371
Transcription
  • Wed Jan 06:  Temp 11 at Kalsos, 12 here & 10 at Wilsons and reported to be zero in Bellingham. Strong wind to day from the north. The Fidalgo in for Crumines & Osbornes oats. Began reading "The Trail of the Lonesome Pine"
  • Fri Jan 08:  Temp 10 at 6 AM. Temp 9 at 10 PM. Mercury rose to 17 degrees once today but Cold winter is still with us. One chicken died from frost. Began reading "The Riverman"
