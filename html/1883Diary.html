1882 Nov 24-Jan 5, 1883

Image Id: 002-0466
Transcription
  • Fri Nov 24:  Spent the day in LaConner. Peter working for Otto. 8 hours.
  • Sat Nov 25:  Came from LaConner. Worked on the bedroom and finished loading the grain. Heavy rain.
  • Sun Nov 26:  Staid at home all day day. Tallied grain for Otto in the granary.
  • Mon Nov 27:  Worked on the bedroom some. Hung door Sat. Went over to Marches to get fruit & beds.
  • Tue Nov 28:  Got ready & started for Seattle. As far as LaConner.
  • Wed Nov 29:  Came to Utsalady last night. Arrived in Seattle at 3.50 P.M. & went to the Occidental Hotel.
  • Thu Nov 30:  Spent the day in Seattle. Went to church.
  • Fri Dec 01:  In Seattle Sat. & Sun. also. Bought an organ & furniture, &c.
  • Sat Dec 02:  No entry. In Seattle.
  • Sun Dec 03:  No entry. In Seattle.
  • Mon Dec 04:  Came as far as LaConner per Str Idaho.
  • Tue Dec 05:  Came home. Arrived in the P.M. Poorer & wiser.
  • Wed Dec 06:  Worked on the bedroom some. Rainy day.
  • Thu Dec 07:  Worked on the bedroom some. Found old Doll cool and in a bad situation. Got her up.
  • Fri Dec 08:  Went to look at some timber land. Was down to Whitneys fixing up engine. Making gates, &c.
  • Sat Dec 09:  Went to LaConner to get cloth & paper for house. Had Callahans boat. Got a bill of Andrews.
  • Sun Dec 10:  Staid at home. Stormy. Old Dolly still bad off.
  • Mon Dec 11:  Stormy. Peter & I worked on the bedrooms putting on cloth.
  • Tue Dec 12:  Putting on paper. The organ came by steamer "Welcome". Settled with Crosby. Took check for 542.82. First snow storm of the season. Old Doll still alive.
  • Wed Dec 13:  Working on bedrooms, papering &c. Went down & tallied & marked Darcys grain for Crosby.
  • Thu Dec 14:  Finished papering the bedrooms.
  • Fri Dec 15:  Caring(?) bedrooms, hanging doors, &c. Peter also.
  • Sat Dec 16:  Painting bedrooms. Peter hanging doors. Stormy day. Had a severe attack of Sciattica.
  • Sun Dec 17:  Not feeling well. Vinz came up in the evening. Ida went down there a little while.
  • Mon Dec 18:  Felt a little better. Painted the sitting room. Peter cleaning out bedroom, &c.
  • Tue Dec 19:  Weather warm & wet. Painting bedrooms. Peter helping Whitney kill hogs. Burden here in the evening.
  • Wed Dec 20:  Still painting. Peter choring around. Cutting wood &c.
  • Thu Dec 21:  Peter & I both working for Vinz making a tank. Warm.
  • Fri Dec 22:  Peter & Ida went to LaConner. I working for Vinz on tank.
  • Sat Dec 23:  Helped Stewart survey in the morning. Filed Vinz saw & trimmed fruit trees some. Ida & Peter came home at 4 oclock. Frost this morning.
  • Sun Dec 24:  Staid at home in the A.M. Went to Vinz's in the P.M. Whitney went to town.
  • Mon Dec 25:  Christmas. Pruned trees & tinkered about the house some.
  • Tue Dec 26:  Went down to Siegfreds Point & cut a tree for wood.
  • Wed Dec 27:  Filed a saw for Kalso. Rainy part of the day. Peter cutting wood, &c.
  • Thu Dec 28:  Began ditching some. Went to LaConner in the P.M. Peter working on the dyke. Frosty.
  • Fri Dec 29:  Came from LaConner with the furniture. Frost. Peter ditching some, working on house in the P.M. Whitewashing.
  • Sat Dec 30:  Working on house. Mercury at 24 this A.M. Papering & putting down carpet &c.
  • Sun Dec 31:  Staid at home all day. Very pleasant day. Allen(?) down here to dinner.
  • Mon Jan 01:  Went over to Jenni's & got a horse "Prince". Did not do much of anything. Peter went up to LaConner to the dance.
  • Tue Jan 02:  Did not do much. Fine frost weather. Nettie sick with cold.
  • Wed Jan 03:  Peter did not work yesterday. Went to to Siegfreds Point to cut stove wood.
  • Thu Jan 04:  Went down to cut wood the same as yesterday. Rainy some to day & a strong S.E. wind.
  • Fri Jan 05:  Raining part of the day. Ditching some. White washing the kitchen in the P.M.

1883 Jan 6-Feb 17

Image Id: 002-0467
Transcription
  • Sat Jan 06:  Ditching some. Rainy in the P.M. Warm.
  • Sun Jan 07:  Did not go any where. George Brown was here and staid all night.
  • Mon Jan 08:  Rainy day. Started for LaConner with Geo. Brown. Got wet some. Staid at McGlinn's.
  • Tue Jan 09:  Staid in LaConner & went to a Temperance lecture in the eve by Col. Hawkins. Rainy & warm.
  • Wed Jan 10:  Came from LaConner in the A.M. & went up to look at some timber land in the P.M. Peter ditching & cutting wood. Whitewashing yesterday.
  • Thu Jan 11:  Peter & I cutting wood.
  • Fri Jan 12:  Went to town to see about filing on timber land. Peter at work in the woods.
  • Sat Jan 13:  Both cutting wood. Had a snow storm. Warm.
  • Sun Jan 14:  Went down to Vinz's a little while in the P.M. Fine weather.
  • Mon Jan 15:  Did not do much in the AM dug ditch in the PM.
  • Tue Jan 16:  Went to LaConner on my way to Olympia for timber land. Cold winter night. Steamer Josephine blew up and killed 8 people.
  • Wed Jan 17:  Went aboard the Idaho. last night and got to Seattle at 7:30 PM then went aboard the G.E. Starr. [margin: 12 degrees.]
  • Thu Jan 18:  Went to Olympia and filed on 40 acres of Timber land. Came back to Seattle & next to bed on the Quincy. Mercury down to 10 degrees this A.M. [margin: Cold 10 degrees.]
  • Fri Jan 19:  Coming home. Got to LaConner at 1.30 A.M. Sat.
  • Sat Jan 20:  Warmer. Started from LaConner & walked home. Peter working for Vinz cutting wood.
  • Sun Jan 21:  Pleasant day. Whitneys folks & Mrs. Alexander up in the P.M. also Vinzy.
  • Mon Jan 22:  Worked around home filing saws, &c. Peter not working in the A.M. Was at the Skagit.
  • Tue Jan 23:  Went up to Jones's place to cut road to timber land.
  • Wed Jan 24:  Same as yesterday.
  • Thu Jan 25:  Rainy in the A.M. went to town in the P.M. Peter ditching.
  • Fri Jan 26:  Went to the timber & chopped 3 cedars. Al Leamers folks came down in the eve. Stormy day.
  • Sat Jan 27:  Peter fixing harness & ditching. Al's folks here yet. Whitneys folks arrived from Cal. this A.M.
  • Sun Jan 28:  Stormy. Al started for LaConner. I went to part way with him.
  • Mon Jan 29:  Hauled out wood. Finished & got home in good season.
  • Tue Jan 30:  Went & got a scow load of wood and wheeled it into the yard in the P.M.
  • Wed Jan 31:  Get home another load of wood. Pleasant weather.
  • Thu Feb 01:  Peter working at harness in the A.M. & ditching in the P.M. I ditching some in the P.M.
  • Fri Feb 02:  Went to the timber & scowed ditch timber. Vinz went along and helped. Jones working on his canal.
  • Sat Feb 03:  Peter & I went to the timber. Frosty.
  • Sun Feb 04:  Pleasant day. Grandma here. Mrs Darcy here. Tillinghasts folks & Aunt Lou here in the evening.
  • Mon Feb 05:  Went to the woods again. Still cold & frosty.
  • Tue Feb 06:  Peter went to the break water marsh. I in the woods.
  • Wed Feb 07:  Peter away yet. I went up and cut down two cedar trees.
  • Thu Feb 08:  Peter away yet. I filing saws. Got a new one yesterday.
  • Fri Feb 09:  Sill cold & frosty both went to the woods to cut ditch stuff.
  • Sat Feb 10:  Mercury down to 14° this morning. Cutting ditch timber.
  • Sun Feb 11:  Clowdy with cold south wind. Ed Stacey & Arthur Darcy here to dinner.
  • Mon Feb 12:  Went over to Jennes to work on his barn. High tides.
  • Tue Feb 13:  Went to the timber cold weather again. Scowing.
  • Wed Feb 14:  Went to the timber again making ditch timber & shake stuff.
  • Thu Feb 15:  Went to the woods as usual. Burned a fir tree down.
  • Fri Feb 16:  Went to the woods as usual. Burned a fir tree down.
  • Sat Feb 17:  Went to Jennes but had to quit for rain. Cold & windy. Split & sawed wood in the P.M. in the woodshed.
