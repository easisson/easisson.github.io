1908 Dec 25-Jan 9, 1909

Image Id: 001-0371
Transcription
  • Wed Jan 06:  Temp 11 at Kalsos, 12 here & 10 at Wilsons and reported to be zero in Bellingham. Strong wind to day from the north. The Fidalgo in for Crumines & Osbornes oats. Began reading "The Trail of the Lonesome Pine"
  • Fri Jan 08:  Temp 10 at 6 AM. Temp 9 at 10 PM. Mercury rose to 17 degrees once today but Cold winter is still with us. One chicken died from frost. Began reading "The Riverman"

1909 Jan 10-28

Image Id: 001-0372
Transcription
  • Sun Jan 10:  Temp 12 and some more snow. Dr. Palmer came last night and went back with out any ducks this Evening. Earthquake at 3.45

1909 Jan 29-Feb 14

Image Id: 001-0373
Transcription
  • Sat Feb 06:  Reading the "Lion and the Mouse"

1909 Mar 9-30

Image Id: 001-0375
Transcription
  • Sat Mar 27:  Gave an order for a Ford auto.

1909 Mar 31-Apr 20

Image Id: 001-0376
Transcription
  • Mon Apr 19:  Grant married Hilda Jorgenson.

1909 May 8-Jun 1

Image Id: 001-0378
Transcription
  • Sat May 15:  Grandson Colvin Allen Wilson born.

1909 Jun 2-26

Image Id: 001-0379
Transcription
  • Sat Jun 12:  Went to AYP (Alaska-Yukon-Pacific Exposition) track meet.
  • Mon Jun 14:  Grant married. All went to wedding.
  • Wed Jun 16:  Got my car this P.M.

1909 Jun 27-Jul 14

Image Id: 001-0380
Transcription
  • Thu Jul 01:  Drove car home from Seattle. 106 miles on 6 gallons of gas.
  • Tue Jul 06:  Mended a puncture in the auto wheel tire.
  • Fri Jul 09:  Got the auto radiator fixed.
  • Sun Jul 11:  772 miles on the auto this AM.

1909 Jul 15-Aug 2

Image Id: 001-0381
Transcription
  • Fri Jul 23:  Car has made 1006 miles.

1909 Sep 11-27

Image Id: 001-0384
Transcription
  • Sat Sep 25:  The auto has now gone 2060 miles.

1909 Sep 28-Oct 13

Image Id: 001-0385
Transcription
  • Fri Oct 01:  At the Fair with Tillinghast and saw and heard Taft.

1909 Oct 14-Nov 1

Image Id: 001-0386
Transcription
  • Sat Oct 23:  Went to LaConner to act as Judge on the Albers Cup Oat Contest. Drove Charley, Roads bad. This is my first trip with a horse since last spring.
  • Wed Oct 27:  Am 60 today. Staid at home and kept quiet. No rain but threatening.

1909 Nov 2-Nov 25

Image Id: 001-0387
Transcription
  • Tue Nov 09:  Ida & self went to Anacortes to look at lots. Did not buy. Found them pretty high, those that we would want.

1909 Nov 26-Dec 9

Image Id: 001-0388
Transcription
  • Tue Nov 30:  Skagit River broke the dyke in a number of places and is flooding the whole country. It will be the worst general flood on record.
  • Wed Dec 08:  Adrian Allen drowned near his home on Samish Island.
