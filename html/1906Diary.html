1906 Feb 26-Mar 11

Image Id: 001-0291
Transcription
  • Sat Mar 03:  Mrs Wharton's novel "House of Myrth"
  • Wed Mar 07:  Warm & pleasant. The boy's plowing in the PM. Hauling stall bottoms in the AM. Self up to Kalso's and Ball's to get money for Famine Fund for Japs.

1906 Mar 12-27

Image Id: 001-0292
Transcription
  • Thu Mar 15:  Self reading most of the time. Finished "Alice of Old Vincennes" also read some of "The Spoilers".

1906 May 29-Jun 16Duplicate of previous

Image Id: 001-0300
Transcription
  • Mon Jun 04:  Self went to La C with Nettie and started for Pullman on the eve train. Got as far as Mt Vernon.
  • Tue Jun 05:  On the way to Spokane. Arrived at 9:30 PM.
  • Wed Jun 06:  Self in Spokane
  • Thu Jun 07:  Self went from Spokane to Pullman
  • Fri Jun 08:  Attending Farmer's meeting
  • Sat Jun 09:  Attending Farmer's meeting
  • Tue Jun 12:  Went to Spokane and out to Hazelwood Dairy Farms
  • Wed Jun 13:  Came home from Spokane

1906 Oct 19-Nov 6

Image Id: 001-0309
Transcription
  • Sat Oct 27:  I am 57 today. Went back to McGinnis's and in PM went with the Dr to see a football game.

1906 Nov 22-Dec 9

Image Id: 001-0311
Transcription
  • Mon Dec 03:  Susie Stacey died this forenoon 11:30. Self, Art & Grant working on the ditch in the A.M. Got up a team & Art bgan plowing in the P.M. Self fixed flower beds in the P.M. & Grant went to B.V. to get Nell shod.
