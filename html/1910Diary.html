1910 Mar 17-Apr 15E.A. trip East

Image Id: 001-0395
Transcription
  • Fri Apr 01:  In Seattle. At J.C. McGinnis for supper. Bot a ticket for New York via S.F., Las A. and New Orleans.
  • Sat Apr 02:  Started for the East at 8:30 at Portland this eve at suppertime.

1910 May 22-Jun 15E.A. trip East

Image Id: 001-0397
Transcription
  • Mon May 23:  Went to Geo Baileys' to dinner & to Scranton to see Buffalo Bill's Show. Then home with Geo & staid all night.
  • Sat May 28:  Went to Scranton & bot a ticket for Seattle $64.50. Took dinner with the Clays & saw the Comet.
  • Wed Jun 01:  Went to La Plume to say good-bye & started for the Pacific Coast. Reaching Buffalo at 8;30.

*1910 May 30-Jul 6Nettie's entries during EA's trip East

Image Id: 001-0402
Transcription
  • Tue May 31:  This is our 34 anniversary. One is spending here alone. the other across the Continent alone

1910 Sep 20-Oct 14

Image Id: 001-0406
Transcription
  • Tue Sep 27:  Birth of grandson Darrell E Sisson

1910 Oct 15-Nov 8

Image Id: 001-0407
Transcription
  • Thu Oct 27:  61 today. Went to La C. to go to Bellingham but gave it up and me and Franks folks went to Anacortes in the PM and looked at the Dodge house.
  • Tue Nov 01:  Went to Anacortes with Ida and bot the Dodge house, lot & furniture.
