1929 Feb 25-Mar 13

Image Id: 003-0063
Transcription
  • Wed Feb 27:  The Retriever and crew went to Seattle.
  • Fri Mar 01:  Bot 50 galons of gasoline at 10 1/2 cts per Galon

1929 Mar 14-Mar 29

Image Id: 003-0064
Transcription
  • Sat Mar 23:  The Retriever loading and sailed at 5 o'clock for Alaska. Frank, Donald and Merlin Wilson aboard.
  • Fri Mar 29:  Frank and crew returned this Eve with leaky boat.

1929 May 24-Jun 9

Image Id: 003-0068
Transcription
  • Tue Jun 04:  Went to Seattle to Pioneer meeting and presided at the meeting this P.M.
  • Fri Jun 07:  Receive letter from Frank. He has 1372 King's and 50 cases of Reds. [salmon]

1929 Jun 29-Jul 16

Image Id: 003-0070
Transcription
  • Thu Jul 11:  Pleasant. At home A.M. In the P.M. sold the old Ford for $175.00 and bot a new Model A Standard Coupe Price $702.00. Took a lessen driving. Got along allright except leaving the garage.

1929 Aug 4-21

Image Id: 003-0072
Transcription
  • Sat Aug 10:  Went to Mt Vernon and had a rumble seat put in the Ford.

1929 Aug 22-Sep 7

Image Id: 003-0073
Transcription
  • Thu Sep 05:  Heard that the Retriever left Ketchikan.

1929 Oct 12-28

Image Id: 003-0076
Transcription
  • Sun Oct 27:  Pleasant. Went to Pearls' for my birthday party. The 80th. All hands went and had an enjoyable time.
